WHERE AM I GOING?

I hope we leave with more than we brought.

Where shall we begin? I am Daniel Schwab, I have been thinking for at least 24 years, and I have decided to put some things up here. The goal of this blog, if that is what we must call it, is to give me the illusion of an audience. Through time, trials, and whatever, I have come to the conclusion that I write best when I am focused on the listener and when I expect to be heard. Either way, I remember them better when I write them down. Thank you for being a part of this process! I hope to put some things up that will give us both something to think about.
