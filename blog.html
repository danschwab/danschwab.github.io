WHERE AM I GOING?

I hope we leave with more than we brought.

Welcome to Daniel Schwab's personal blog. Through time, trials, and stuff I have come to some conclusions and many more questions. I come from a family of academic intellectuals. We love the pursuit of beauty and truth, but often fall into the trap of thinking too highly of our own opinions. As I publish articles here, I recognize that I am not an expert. In truth, I am often ignorant, naive, and hypocritical. I do not hope to win the war of ideas raging in our century by sharing my wisdom. Instead, I hope to practice humbly engaging with these ideas. I hope to pursue beauty and truth with common sense and my experience. I hope to put some things up that will give us both something to think about, and God knows where we will end up.
